About Me

As a dedicated Mechanical Engineer at Upper Syange Hydropower Limited in Nepal, I bring 19 months of hands-on experience in supervising hydropower plant operations and designing critical plant components. My passion lies in developing sustainable energy solutions that harness the natural power of water to bring clean electricity to communities across Nepal.

I graduated from Visvesvaraya Technological University in Bangalore, India in 2022, where I developed strong foundations in mechanical engineering principles, CAD software (AutoCAD, SolidWorks), and problem-solving methodologies. My role involves supervising plant operators, inspecting hydropower facilities, and coordinating with government organizations to efficiently evacuate generated energy to the grid.

With expertise in plant design, operations management, and a commitment to excellence, I work to ensure optimal performance of hydropower infrastructure while contributing to Nepal's renewable energy goals. I believe in continuous learning, integrity, and building strong relationships to deliver the best results for my organization and community.
